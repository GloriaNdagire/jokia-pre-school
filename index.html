WE :PLAY, LEARN AND GROW.


Jokia Pre-School is a nursery school and kindergarten located in Kampala - Uganda. The motto of the school is PLAY. LEARN. GROW. The school aims to give a great foundation for a child through holistic learning, all-round curricular and extracurricular activities and a personal touch for each individual learner. Can you share the values that Jokia Pre-School upholds? Respect, Responsibility and Kindness Jokia Pre-School has great teachers, a large space for kids to play and appreciate the outside world plus a wide-range of activities for a child to engage in and learn from. What is the unique selling point of Jokia Pre-School?
An urban style education with personalised learning
